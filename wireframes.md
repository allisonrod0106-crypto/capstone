## Homesreen
The landing screen showing the menu. 

## Daily Journal
The main writing view where users respond to the day's prompt, free-write, set intentions, and log gratitude

## Free Write
A distraction-free, blank canvas mode for unstructured, pressure-free writing. 

## Calender
A monthly overview of past entries, with visual indicators showing which days were logged and entry length at a glance.

## Settings 
Where users customize their experience, including prompt preferences, timer preferences, daily reminders, streak goals, and theme options.
